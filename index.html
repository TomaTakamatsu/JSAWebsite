Our Four Pillars

Building Community, Cultivating Knowledge, Celebrating Culture, and Ensuring Sustainability

Foster Community

Our primary goal is to establish a strong and secure community where students with shared backgrounds and interests can come together to celebrate and explore Japanese culture.

Promote Comprehensive Learning

We aim to go beyond the specific aspects of Japanese culture covered by existing organizations, striving to create a more inclusive platform for comprehensive learning and understanding of all its diverse components.

Curate Diverse Events

We are committed to organizing a wide range of events that strike a harmonious balance between cultural and educational activities, as well as enjoyable social gatherings, ensuring a vibrant and engaging experience for our members.

Secure Sustainability

In the long term, we aim to establish a robust fundraising mechanism to ensure the financial sustainability of our club events, facilitating a vibrant and enriching experience for our members.

INTERN PROGRAM

How does it work?

First things first, we're thrilled to have you on board. So, you want to join our intern program? That's fantastic! The first step is as simple as it gets - sign up! Send in your application, and tell us a bit about yourself. We want to know why you're interested and what you bring to the table.

Now, after you've made it in, it's time to get your hands dirty. You'll be shadowing our experienced board members, like getting backstage access. This is where you'll really get to know the ropes and see how we make the magic happen. Don't hesitate to shoot questions our way; we're here to help and share what we know.

Once you've soaked up that knowledge, it's your chance to shine. Consider applying for a board position - this is where you get to put your stamp on things. Whether you aim for a leadership role or want to bring your unique ideas to the table, this is your platform to showcase your potential.
